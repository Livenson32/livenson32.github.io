# 🖼️ image-compressor - Compress Your Images Easily

## 👋 Overview
Welcome to the image-compressor! This application allows you to compress images quickly and privately. It is designed for batch processing, which means you can compress multiple images at once. With our use of WebAssembly, the performance is fast and efficient.
